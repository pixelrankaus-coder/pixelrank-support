## ARTICLE 2: How to Submit a Support Ticket

**Category:** Tickets
**Status:** PUBLISHED
**Excerpt:** Step-by-step guide to creating a support ticket and getting help from our team.

### Content (HTML):

```html
<div class="help-article">
  <h2>Submitting a Support Ticket</h2>
  <p>Need help? Our support team is here for you. Follow these simple steps to submit a ticket and get assistance.</p>

  <h3>Step 1: Navigate to New Ticket</h3>
  <p>You can create a new ticket in several ways:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Click the <strong>"New Ticket"</strong> button in the welcome banner on your dashboard</li>
    <li>Click <strong>"Submit a Ticket"</strong> in the Quick Actions section</li>
    <li>Go to <strong>My Tickets</strong> and click the <strong>"New Ticket"</strong> button</li>
  </ul>

  </div>

  <h3>Step 2: Fill Out the Ticket Form</h3>
  <p>The ticket form has three fields:</p>

  <div style="background: #fff; border: 1px solid #e5e7eb; border-radius: 8px; padding: 20px; margin: 20px 0;">
    <h4 style="margin-top: 0; color: #1f2937;">Subject (Required)</h4>
    <p style="color: #6b7280; margin-bottom: 16px;">Write a brief, clear description of your issue. Good subjects help our team understand your problem quickly.</p>

    <div style="display: flex; gap: 20px; flex-wrap: wrap;">

        <p style="color: #16a34a; font-weight: 600; margin-bottom: 8px;">Good Examples:</p>

          <li>"Cannot login to my account"</li>
          <li>"Invoice #1234 shows wrong amount"</li>
          <li>"Need help setting up integration"</li>

      </div>
      <div style="flex: 1; min-width: 200px;">
        <p style="color: #dc2626; font-weight: 600; margin-bottom: 8px;">Avoid:</p>
        <ul style="color: #6b7280; margin: 0;">
          <li>"Help!"</li>
          <li>"Problem"</li>
          <li>"Urgent!!!"</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div style="background: #fff; border: 1px solid #e5e7eb; border-radius: 8px; padding: 20px; margin: 20px 0;">

    <p style="color: #6b7280;">Select the urgency level that best matches your situation:</p>

    <table style="width: 100%; border-collapse: collapse; margin-top: 12px;">
      <tr>
        <td style="padding: 8px 12px; background: #f0fdf4; border-radius: 4px;">
          <strong style="color: #166534;">Low</strong>
        </td>
        <td style="padding: 8px 12px;">General questions, feedback, or minor issues that aren't time-sensitive</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td style="padding: 8px 12px; background: #fefce8; border-radius: 4px;">
          <strong style="color: #854d0e;">Medium</strong>
        </td>
        <td style="padding: 8px 12px;">Issues affecting your work but with workarounds available</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td style="padding: 8px 12px; background: #fef2f2; border-radius: 4px;">
          <strong style="color: #991b1b;">High</strong>
        </td>
        <td style="padding: 8px 12px;">Significant problems affecting your daily work</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td style="padding: 8px 12px; background: #fef2f2; border-radius: 4px;">
          <strong style="color: #7f1d1d;">Urgent</strong>
        </td>
        <td style="padding: 8px 12px;">Critical issues causing complete work stoppage or data loss</td>
      </tr>
    </table>
  </div>

  <div style="background: #fff; border: 1px solid #e5e7eb; border-radius: 8px; padding: 20px; margin: 20px 0;">

    <p style="color: #6b7280;">Provide detailed information about your issue. The more details you include, the faster we can help you.</p>

    <p style="font-weight: 600; margin-top: 16px; margin-bottom: 8px;">Include in your description:</p>
    <ul style="color: #6b7280;">
      <li>What were you trying to do?</li>
      <li>What happened instead?</li>
      <li>Any error messages you saw (copy the exact text)</li>
      <li>Steps to reproduce the problem</li>
      <li>When did the issue start?</li>
      <li>Have you tried any troubleshooting steps?</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <!-- IMAGE PLACEHOLDER -->

  <h3>Step 3: Submit Your Ticket</h3>
  <p>Once you've filled out the form:</p>
  <ol>
    <li>Review your information to make sure it's accurate</li>
    <li>Click the <strong>"Submit Ticket"</strong> button</li>
    <li>You'll be redirected to your new ticket's page</li>
    <li>You'll also receive a confirmation email</li>
  </ol>

  <div style="background: #f0f9ff; border: 1px solid #bae6fd; border-radius: 8px; padding: 16px; margin: 20px 0;">
    <strong>What happens next?</strong><br>
    Our support team will review your ticket and respond as quickly as possible. You'll receive an email notification when there's a reply, and you can also check for updates in the portal.
  </div>

  <h3>Tips for Faster Resolution</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Be specific</strong> - Vague descriptions lead to back-and-forth questions</li>
    <li><strong>Include screenshots</strong> - If applicable, attach images showing the issue</li>
    <li><strong>One issue per ticket</strong> - This helps us track and resolve each problem efficiently</li>
    <li><strong>Check existing tickets</strong> - Make sure you haven't already reported the same issue</li>
  </ul>
</div>
```

## ARTICLE 4: Responding to Tickets

**Category:** Tickets
**Status:** PUBLISHED
**Excerpt:** How to reply to support tickets, add attachments, and communicate with our team.

### Content (HTML):

```html
<div class="help-article">
  <h2>Responding to Your Support Tickets</h2>
  <p>Effective communication helps us resolve your issues faster. Here's how to reply to tickets and provide the information we need.</p>

  <h3>Replying to a Ticket</h3>
  <p>When you open a ticket, you'll see the conversation history and a reply box at the bottom:</p>

  <ol>
    <li><strong>Navigate to the ticket</strong> - Go to My Tickets and click on the ticket you want to respond to</li>
    <li><strong>Scroll to the reply box</strong> - You'll find it at the bottom of the conversation</li>
    <li><strong>Type your message</strong> - Enter your response in the text area</li>
    <li><strong>Click "Send Reply"</strong> - Your message will be added to the conversation</li>
  </ol>

  <!-- IMAGE PLACEHOLDER -->
  <div style="background: #f3f4f6; border: 2px dashed #d1d5db; border-radius: 8px; padding: 40px; text-align: center; margin: 20px 0;">
    <p style="color: #6b7280; margin: 0;">[Screenshot: Reply box at the bottom of a ticket conversation]</p>
  </div>

  <h3>Adding Attachments</h3>
  <p>Sometimes screenshots or files can help explain your issue better:</p>

  <ol>
    <li>Click the <strong>attachment icon</strong> (paperclip) in the reply box</li>
    <li>Select one or more files from your computer</li>
    <li>Wait for the files to upload (you'll see a progress indicator)</li>
    <li>The attachments will appear below the text area</li>
    <li>Click "Send Reply" to submit your message with attachments</li>
  </ol>

  <div style="background: #fff; border: 1px solid #e5e7eb; border-radius: 8px; padding: 20px; margin: 20px 0;">
    <h4 style="margin-top: 0;">Supported File Types</h4>

        <td style="padding: 8px; width: 40%;"><strong>Images</strong></td>
        <td style="padding: 8px; color: #6b7280;">JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td style="padding: 8px;"><strong>Documents</strong></td>
        <td style="padding: 8px; color: #6b7280;">PDF, Word (.doc, .docx), Excel (.xls, .xlsx)</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td style="padding: 8px;"><strong>Text Files</strong></td>
        <td style="padding: 8px; color: #6b7280;">TXT, CSV, JSON</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td style="padding: 8px;"><strong>Archives</strong></td>

      <p style="color: #6b7280; margin: 8px 0 0 0;">Instead of "It's still not working," describe exactly what happens. For example: "When I click the Save button, I see an error message that says 'Connection timeout'"</p>
    </div>

    <div style="background: #f0fdf4; border: 1px solid #bbf7d0; border-radius: 8px; padding: 16px;">
      <strong style="color: #166534;">Include Context</strong>

    <div style="background: #f0fdf4; border: 1px solid #bbf7d0; border-radius: 8px; padding: 16px;">
      <strong style="color: #166534;">Respond Promptly</strong>
      <p style="color: #6b7280; margin: 8px 0 0 0;">If we've asked for information, try to respond as soon as you can. This keeps your ticket active and helps us resolve it faster.</p>

    </div>
  </div>

  <p>You'll receive email notifications when:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Our team replies to your ticket</li>
```
